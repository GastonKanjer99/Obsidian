- A prone creature’s only [movement](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Movement?expansion=34047#content) option is to [crawl](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Movement?expansion=34047#toc_5), unless it stands up and thereby ends the condition.
- The creature has [disadvantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2) on [attack rolls](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Combat?expansion=34047#toc_32).
- An [attack roll](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Combat?expansion=34047#toc_32) against the creature has [advantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2) if the attacker is within 5 feet of the creature. Otherwise, the attack roll has [disadvantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2).
