- A restrained creature’s [speed](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Movement?expansion=34047#toc_1) becomes 0, and it can’t benefit from any bonus to its speed.
- [Attack rolls](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Combat?expansion=34047#toc_32) against the creature have [advantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2), and the creature’s attack rolls have [disadvantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2).
- The creature has [disadvantage](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_2) on [Dexterity](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_17) [saving throws](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Ability%20Scores?expansion=34047#toc_35).
